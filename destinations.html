Explore Nepal's Top Destinations

Kathmandu

Explore the vibrant capital city of Nepal, where history and culture blend throughout anciet temples and bustling markets. Home to UNESCO world heritage sites like Hanumandhoka Durbar Square and Swoyambhunath, Kathmandu is a must for those who want a mix of urban energy and traditional Nepali charm.

Pokhara

Known as Nepal's tourism capital, Pokhara is the pinnacle of Nepal's natural beuaty. Nestled beside the Phewa Lake, it is the gateway to the Annapurna region. Famous for adventure spots, paragliding, and serene views, it's perfect for travelers seeking both thrill and tranquility.

Chitwan

Love nature and wildlife? Then Chitwan National Park is just the place for you. From the rare one-horned rhinoceros to the Bengal tiger, enjoy watching various animals and exotic birds on an unforgettable jungle safari. Truly a must-visit for adventure seekers.

Lumbini

As the birthplace of Lord Buddha, Lumbini is a spiritual heaven. Walk among the sacred monasteries, ancient monuments, temples and the peaceful meditation gardens. Lumbini offers spirituality, reflection, and history in your journey.

Everest Region

Home to the world's tallest peak, the Everest region attracts trekkers and mountaineers from all over the world. Want to climb the peak or simply bask in the serene nature? We've got you covered. Explore Namche Bazar, Sagarmatha National Park and stunning Himalayn views for a life-changing adventure.

Annapurna Trek

One of the most popular trekking circuits in the world, the Annapurna trek offers diverse landscapes, charming villages, and spectacular mountain views. Suitale for both beginner and experienced trekkers looking for a Nepalese adventure.
